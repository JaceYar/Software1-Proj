# PRD: Combined Hotel & Store Reservation System ## Project Overview A unified digital platform for a unique establishment that combines a themed luxury hotel with an exclusive retail store. ## Core Features ### 1. Hotel Reservations * **Themed Floors:** * **Nature Retreat:** Nature-inspired (Single, Double, Family). * **Urban Elegance:** Modern & Elegant (Suite, Deluxe). * **Vintage Charm:** Classic touch (Standard, Deluxe). * **Room Attributes:** Unique number, bed types (Twin, Full, Queen, King), smoking/non-smoking. * **Quality Levels:** Executive, Business, Comfort, Economy (each with max daily rates). * **Pricing:** Supports promotional, group, and non-refundable rates. * **Booking Flow:** Date selection -> Availability check -> Room selection -> Guest info (Name, Address, CC). * **Management:** * Modify/Cancel reservations. * Cancellation Policy: Free within 2 days; 80% of one night after 2 days. * Check-in/Check-out tracking. * Corporate Billing: Direct billing to corporations for business guests. ### 2. Integrated Store * **Offerings:** Clothing, accessories, local artisanal goods. * **Experience:** Seamless browsing for hotel guests. ### 3. Managerial Tools * **Reporting:** Summary of all reservations and payments. * **Financial Tracking:** Revenue and billing status. ## User Personas * **Leisure Guest:** Looking for a unique, themed stay and shopping. * **Corporate Guest:** Requires efficient booking and direct company billing. * **Hotel Manager:** Needs high-level oversight of operations and finances.
